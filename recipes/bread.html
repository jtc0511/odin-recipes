Bread

homemade bread is great because it offers superior quality, freshness, flavor, and customization while allowing you to take control of the ingredients and enjoy the satisfying process of baking. It's a wholesome and rewarding culinary experience that can become a cherished tradition in many households.

ingrediants

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour (you can also use bread flour for a slightly better texture)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (around 110째F or 43째C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or olive oil)

instructions

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. This indicates that the yeast is active.
  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
  3. Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients: Create a well in the center of the dry ingredients. Pour in the activated yeast mixture and the vegetable oil. Stir everything together with a wooden spoon or a mixer with a dough hook attachment until the dough comes together.
  4. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If it's too sticky, add a little more flour as needed.
  5. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  6. Shape the Loaf: After the first rise, punch down the dough to remove excess air. Shape it into a loaf by rolling it out into a rectangle and then rolling it up tightly. Place the shaped dough into a greased 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) loaf pan.
  7. Second Rise: Cover the loaf pan with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and allow it to rise again for about 30-45 minutes, or until it's puffed up and reaches just above the rim of the pan.
  8. Preheat and Bake: Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C). Once preheated, remove the cover from the loaf pan and bake the bread for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the bread sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  9. Cool and Enjoy: Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice and enjoy your homemade bread!
